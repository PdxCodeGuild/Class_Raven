Mission
The mission of Green Box is to provide 100% recyled boxes completely free. We collect the cardboard ourselves routinely have personnel at local businesses in order to connect to the communites we serve.
How to Donate:
You can donate to our cause by bringing your cardboard to one of our supported locations and by also by donating money through our donation links located in our Contact section below
