Félix Lengyel

Félix Lengyel was born on 12th November 1995. He is a famous figure who is popularly known by his online name xQc.
He is a popular Twitch streamer, internet identity, and former professional esports player. Felix was apart of the team Dallas Fuel in Overwatch League.
He now spends his time on Twitch as a "variety" streamer. Streaming on average, 8 hours a day.
