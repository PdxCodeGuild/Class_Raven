Leeroy Wiki

Leeroy Jenkins is a player character created by Ben Schulz in Blizzard Entertainment's MMORPG World of Warcraft.
The character became popular in 2005 from his role in a viral video of game footage where, having been absent during
his group's discussion of a meticulous plan, Leeroy returns and ruins it by charging straight into combat while shouting
his own name as a battle cry. A video which captured the character's antics subsequently went viral and became an
Internet meme. As a result of positive reception to the meme, Blizzard subsequently added Leeroy Jenkins into World
of Warcraft as an official non player character, as well as the online card game Hearthstone.
