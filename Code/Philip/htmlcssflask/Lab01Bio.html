Who is Rick Astley?

Rick Astley was only 21 when the single "Never Gonna Give You Up" topped the charts in 1987. The electronic beat, short pompadour haircut, and awkward dance moves made it instantly cringworthy. The song aged as well as a glass of milk, unitl it really took off as a meme in 2007.

What is Rickrolling?

Rickrolling started in 2007 on 4chan to prank people to click on a link for "Grand Theft Auto IV" which would instead play the song.

Where is Rick now?

Rick looks much the same as he did in 1987, living in the UK, and enjoying his newfound fame.
