Small Introduction

Dwayne Douglas Johnson was born in Hayward, California[29] on May 2, 1972,[30] the son of Ata Johnson (née Maivia; born 1948)[31] and former professional wrestler Rocky Johnson (born Wayde Douglas Bowles; 1944–2020).[32][33] Growing up, Johnson lived briefly in Grey Lynn in New Zealand with his mother's family,[34] where he played rugby[35] and attended Richmond Road Primary School before returning to the U.S.[34] He attended Montclaire Elementary School in Charlotte, North Carolina, before moving to Hamden, Connecticut, where he spent a couple of years at Shepherd Glen Elementary School and Hamden Middle School.[36][37] He attended President William McKinley High School in Honolulu, Hawaii, then Glencliff High School and McGavock High School in Nashville, Tennessee, and finally Freedom High School in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania.[38] Before the age of 17, he struggled, being arrested multiple times for fighting, theft, and check fraud.[39] A gifted athlete, he was on his high schools' gridiron football, track and field, and wrestling teams.[32]
